The Creatix Innovation Process

A proven path to digital success in five phases.

01

Discovery & Strategy

We begin with intensive workshops to understand your challenges, goals, and existing ecosystem. We define the project scope, technical requirements, and strategic roadmap.

02

Design & Prototyping

Our UX/UI experts create wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes. This phase focuses on user journeys, accessibility, and visual aesthetics, ensuring the final product is intuitive.

03

Development & Testing

Agile teams build the solution using best-in-class technologies. Continuous integration and rigorous QA testing cycles ensure a stable, high-performance, and bug-free application.

04

Deployment & Launch

We handle the smooth transition to production, including cloud setup, data migration, and staff training, culminating in a successful, on-time market launch.

05

Support & Evolution

Post-launch, we provide continuous monitoring, maintenance, and ongoing support. We partner with you to plan future enhancements and feature additions based on performance data.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries.

What technologies do you specialize in? +

Our core stack includes React, Angular, Node.js (MERN/MEAN), Python/Django, and various cloud platforms like AWS and Google Cloud. We utilize modern frameworks for reliable and scalable solutions.

How long does a typical project take? +

Project timelines vary significantly based on complexity. A standard website takes 4-8 weeks, while a custom ERP or complex mobile app can range from 3 to 9 months. We provide detailed timelines after the Discovery phase (Step 1 of our process).

Are your Enterprise Systems customizable? +

Absolutely. Our ERP, CRM, and HRMS systems are designed as bespoke, modular solutions. They are fully customizable to fit your unique business logic and industry compliance standards, not just off-the-shelf software.

Do you provide post-launch maintenance? +

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages, including bug fixes, security updates, performance monitoring, and continuous feature enhancements, ensuring your digital asset remains optimized.
